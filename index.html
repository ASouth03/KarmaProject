What is Karma?

The search for reliable WiFi can be frustrating. During your commute, at your local coffee shop, at the airport or hotel.

Meet Karma: you pocket's new pal. Karma will keep your devices online, everywhere you go.

Karma grabs a 4G signal and creates you own personal WiFi network. Give it a home in your bag and stay connected for good.
